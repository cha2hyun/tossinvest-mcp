# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Project participants must provide a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free environment
regardless of experience, identity, background, nationality, disability, appearance, religion,
sexual orientation, gender identity, or other personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and code rather than attacking people.
- Give actionable feedback and accept correction gracefully.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing personal or credential information.
- Disclose conflicts of interest that affect technical or financial recommendations.
- Treat live-trading safety concerns with urgency and care.
- Remove real account data and credentials from reports, examples, screenshots, and logs.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, stalking, or sexualized attention
- Personal insults, trolling, or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Encouraging users to bypass authentication, approvals, limits, or other safeguards
- Deliberately presenting unsafe financial actions as guaranteed or risk-free
- Using project spaces to promote fraud, market manipulation, or credential theft

## Scope

This policy applies to repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, release channels,
private project communications, and public representation of the project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `cha2hyun.dev@gmail.com`. Include links or context, but remove
credentials and unnecessary personal information.

Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue warnings, restrict participation, or permanently ban
participants depending on severity and recurrence. Reports will be handled as confidentially and
fairly as practical. Retaliation against a reporter is prohibited.
